Stratoterra Design System

Ticker & Alert Bar

Specification for the persistent news ticker component. Three severity tiers control visual weight, display duration, and position — ensuring critical geopolitical events demand attention while routine monitoring flows continuously.

Alert Tiers
CRITICAL
Breaking / Imminent Threat
Display: PRIMARY BAR (44px)
Duration: 30s per alert
Animation: Pulsing background + dot
Sound: Optional alert tone
Priority: Overrides warning tier
Examples: Active military strikes, coups, nuclear tests, major sanctions announced
WARNING
Escalation / High Impact
Display: PRIMARY BAR (44px)
Duration: 30s per alert
Animation: Static background
Sound: None
Priority: Yields to critical
Examples: Troop deployments, trade deal collapses, political instability spikes, energy supply disruptions
WATCH
Monitoring / Developments
Display: SCROLL BAR (30px)
Duration: CONTINUOUS scroll
Animation: Horizontal marquee
Sound: None
Priority: Always visible below primary
Examples: GDP updates, election polls, diplomatic meetings, policy changes, market movements

Component Anatomy
Ticker Bar Structure
1
Severity Tag — Left-aligned label with severity color. Contains pulsing dot (critical only) + tier name. Fixed width, never truncated. Font: Outfit 700, 11px, 2px tracking.
2
Primary Content Area — Alert headline in uppercase. Background uses severity color at 12% opacity with matching top border. Font: Outfit 500, 13px, 0.8px tracking. Single line, truncated with ellipsis if overflow.
3
Timestamp — Right-aligned UTC time in monospace. Updates in real-time. Font: JetBrains Mono 400, 10px. Inherits tier color at 50% opacity.
4
Watch Scroll Bar — Continuous horizontal marquee. Multiple items separated by diamond glyphs. Always visible beneath primary bar. Speed: ~45s full cycle. Font: Outfit 400, 11px, 0.6px tracking.
5
Watch Tag — Small label on left of scroll bar. Blue accent color at 8% opacity background. Font: Outfit 600, 9px, 2px tracking.
